GRANDMA HAMMOND'S CINNAMON ROLLS

No Hammond kitchen should be without Grandma's famous cinnamon roll recipe! Do you see her at the kitchen table with the big yellow bowl full of dough? What lucky kids you are!

Ingredients

  • 2 Cups warm water
  • 2 Tbsp. yeast
  • 3/4 Cup sugar
  • 1 Tbsp. salt
  • 2 eggs
  • 2/3 Cup oil

LASAGNA

This is the dish that Brad has perfected to an art. Casual aquaintances accuse him of pretending to make it and hiring the chef in the closet. We know better. He also made the garlic toast famous along with it.

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs. groud beef
  • 1 8oz. Package lasagna noodles
  • 1 Cup water
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 lb. mozzarella cheese (grated)
  • 1 24oz. Tub cottage cheese
  • 1 Tbsp. Parsley
